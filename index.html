About SMIT

Saylani Mass IT Training (SMIT)

Saylani Mass IT Training (SMIT) is a prominent Pakistani initiative by Saylani Welfare International Trust, offering free or affordable, high-quality, practical IT training in fields like AI, Web Development, and Graphic Design. It focuses on bridging the digital divide, aiming for high employability, job-readiness, and tech education for under-privileged students, boasting 150+ startups and a 70% success rate.

Saylani Mass IT Training (SMIT)

Key Aspects of SMIT (Saylani Mass IT Training)

  • Mission: Empower individuals through modern IT education, viewing tech skills as a right, not a privilege.
  • Courses: Covers various fields including Modern Web Application Development, Digital Marketing with AI, Front End Development, and Networking.
  • Focus: Practical application and hands-on projects, ensuring job readiness.
  • Initiative: An extension of the welfare organization Saylani Welfare International Trust.
